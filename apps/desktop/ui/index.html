Kith

Your circle of devices, in sync.

Kith keeps your notes and memory the same across all your own devices — directly, device to device.

There's no account to create and no company server holding your data, because there isn't one. Devices link with a short code, and everything is end-to-end encrypted on the way and encrypted on disk.

Alpha software. Built and tested locally, not yet independently reviewed. Lovely for everyday notes; hold off on anything you couldn't bear to lose just yet.
