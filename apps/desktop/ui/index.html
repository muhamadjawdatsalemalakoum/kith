Your spaces

A space is a private, end-to-end-encrypted world of its own — its own memory, tabs, and files. Keep a Personal space for yourself, or make a Team space with roles for a trusted circle. The active space is the one your apps and your AI work in.

The active space is what your connected AI sees over MCP — an agent can't reach any other space. To move a space to another device (or recover one), export it to an encrypted file and import it there. There's no account: if you lose every device without an export, that space's data is gone.

Kith

Your circle of devices, in sync.

Kith keeps your notes and memory the same across all your own devices — directly, device to device.

There's no account to create and no company server holding your data, because there isn't one. Devices link with a short code, and everything is end-to-end encrypted on the way and encrypted on disk.

Alpha software. Built and tested locally, not yet independently reviewed. Lovely for everyday notes; hold off on anything you couldn't bear to lose just yet.
